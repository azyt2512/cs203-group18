How to do perform simulations

Procedure

PROCEDURE FOR SIMULATION

Step 1: First turn on the VCC.

Step 2: Click on the “input” buttons A0, A1, A2 & A3 to give the input.

    Green-(Input 1)

    Red -(Input 0)

Step 3: Now select desired fault position marked at various positions. These are tiny blue circles. If any blue circle turns red that means that part of wire is stucked at1

Step 4: Repeat 2 and 3 to get all the faulty output at the current fault position.

Step 5: Now compare it with the real truth table and try to get the fault position.

Step 6: To change the fault position, click on “Change fault position”
